About

I've been coding since I did a week-long HTML summer camp in 1999 (thanks Mom!) - now I'm freelancing and solving problems for people like you.

You need a new website built from the ground up? No problem. Whether you need a personal blog, an e-commerce ready website for a small business, or a larger presence for your start up, I can help. Let's schedule a call and talk about your needs and what you like design-wise.

Or maybe you have a website you like that needs a tune-up under the hood. I'm a good teamplayer and am happy to help out your in-house developer.

I have been with the small-business with too-little budget and the scrappy non-profit that needed a better website than we could afford. If you're doing something cool, I want to find a way to work with you.
